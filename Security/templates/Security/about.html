WHO WE ARE

SECURIFY is an online web server that detects potentially compromising information in professional and personal emails. In the today's world, internet security is more important than ever, yet many do not understand the potential risks malicious third-party users monopolize on. Our hope is that, after using our services, individuals will be better equipped to protect valuable information.

SECURIFY is powered by the Django web framework to fully integrate our algorithms and website user-interface. By blending HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python, SECURIFY uses a diverse set of languages to create a powerful and beautiful site.
